### DAY 7 语料

1. a tough spot -> 处境艰难、困境
2. with gusto -> 满腔热情
3. rise above -> 克服
4. conform to -> 遵守、符合
5. work overtime -> 加班
6. make for -> 有助于、导致
7. be conducive to -> 有利于、有助于
8. put up with -> 忍受
9. be sb's highest priority -> 成为某人的首要
10. without doing..., sb has no hope of doing -> 没有...就不可能做到
