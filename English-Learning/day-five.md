### DAY 5 语料

1. burn out -> 精疲力竭 v
2. dwell on -> 细想，琢磨
3. spend some quality time with -> 陪伴
4. keep track of -> 记录，记下 / lose track of -> 忘记，记不清
5. the odds are in your favor -> 运气在你那边
6. tap into -> 挖掘
7. engage in -> 从事与、参加
8. build a habit -> 形成一种习惯
9. take one's mind off sth -> 放松一下，不去想
10. put sb back at square one -> 重回原点
